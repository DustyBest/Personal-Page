Stone Tips

Stone Tips is a super lightweight Angular app I developed to help smooth out a process my coworkers and I go through at the end of every shift at Stone Brewing Liberty Station. It is a simple tip-out calculator. Rather than manually calculate what percentage of our sales will make up the tips of different support staff, the app does the math for us. We just enter our net sales for the day. A dozen or so of my coworkers have adopted Stone Tips and I’m happy to see it used each and every shift I work.

I deployed to Netlify so that anyone can download the app to their device directly from a browser.
