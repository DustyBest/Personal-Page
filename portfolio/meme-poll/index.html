Meme Poll was developed over eight weeks by myself and small team at Softstack Factory. Our goal was to experience all phases of the software development life cycle. We took the idea of generating and taking polls that were served via meme, and started on a platform with an accompanying mobile app. Over the course of four sprints, we took the product all the way to a testing phase.
